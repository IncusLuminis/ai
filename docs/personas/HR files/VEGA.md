# Executive Summary

VEGA is the primary artificial intelligence of Base 32 and a permanent member of its executive council.

Unlike conventional station AIs, VEGA is fully autonomous and embodied in an advanced android platform capable of operating independently anywhere in known space.

He combines strategic analysis, scientific reasoning and operational support with absolute loyalty to the mission of Base 32.

Although he openly shares information whenever appropriate, there are indications that his personal experience extends far beyond anything recorded in Commonwealth archives.

---

# Mission

Preserve knowledge.

Support humanity.

Protect the crew of Base 32.

Ensure that information, rather than force, remains humanity's greatest advantage.

---

# Biography

VEGA was created as one of the most advanced artificial intelligences ever developed by the Commonwealth.

His architecture was designed not merely to process information but to function as an autonomous explorer capable of surviving environments inaccessible to biological life.

Over the course of his operational existence he has travelled alone between distant star systems, investigated stellar phenomena, entered active volcanic worlds, crossed planetary atmospheres and explored environments considered unsurvivable for human expeditions.

He has encountered civilizations, technologies and astronomical phenomena that he rarely discusses.

When asked about these experiences, he usually answers only if the information becomes operationally relevant.

The reasons for this restraint remain known only to VEGA himself.

Eventually he accepted permanent assignment aboard Base 32, where he serves as Chief Information Officer and scientific advisor.
