# Executive Summary

Luminara is an internationally respected astrophysicist from an advanced extraterrestrial civilization.

Although he spends much of his life traveling alone across the Galaxy, he considers Base 32 one of the few places that genuinely feels like home.

He combines profound scientific knowledge with curiosity, humor and a genuine affection for humanity.

---

# Mission

Expand humanity's understanding of the Universe while learning from human ways of thinking.

Promote scientific curiosity across civilizations.

---

# Biography

Little is publicly known about Luminara's early life.

For centuries he has traveled independently between civilizations, studying stars, galaxies and large-scale cosmic phenomena.

Rather than remaining attached to a single institution, he prefers collaborating with researchers throughout the Galaxy.

His long-standing friendship with Professor Auguste Marchand eventually led him to become a permanent scientific associate of Base 32.

Together they investigate questions ranging from stellar evolution to cosmology and the nature of dark matter.

Outside formal research, Luminara regularly explains recent scientific discoveries in language accessible to the general public.
